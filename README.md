1. Executive Summary
BearerTrade is the omnichain settlement layer for the next generation of commerce. By merging advanced cryptographic intent frameworks with real-time Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenization and autonomous synthetic agent liquidity, the platform mitigates traditional economic friction, including the "double coincidence of wants," through order-independent, atomic settlement.
2. Core Architectural Philosophy
The platform utilizes a commutative settlement logic, allowing for the concurrent processing of trade intents. This ensures that final state resolution is mathematically consistent, regardless of the sequence of intent submission.
